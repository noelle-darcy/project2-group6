Boarding

Your dog’s home-away-from-home is complete with climate controlled, private guestrooms for eating and sleeping. Unlike traditional kennels, Muttel 6’s dog boarding offers the amenities of two days of daycare plus a night of boarding.

Day Care

Our doggy day care allows for your pups to socialize with other dogs in the same size range. We have Water Wednesdays where we allow the pups to play in the pool. We make sure every pup gets plenty of exercise so they'll be ready to sleep when they go home.

Grooming

We offer three different types of grooming sessions. The Basic Bath includes a bath, blowdry and brush out. The Luxury Bath also includes an ear cleaning, blueberry facial and nail clipping and the Full Groom also comes with a cut and style!
